Shake It Up!

Shake It Up!

|
Live Demo
|
Github

'Shake It Up!' is a mobile-friendly progressive web application allowing users to discover existing cocktails or build their own through a series of guided steps. 'Build Your Own' starts with a base liquor of the user's choice, and prompts them to continuously add their favorite ingredients until they are satisfied with the resulting list of cocktails. At any time, they can click into a specific drink to learn more. The user also has the option to search or browse the entire list of cocktails or ingredients.


Technologies used: Mongo DB, Express.js, React / Redux, Node.js, HTML5 and CSS3 / SASS.
